# Noryn Utilities
     
**Description**  
> - A powerful suite of **Roblox/Luau utility scripts** for encoding, decoding, manipulation, and file management.  
> - All scripts are designed for **ease of use** and come with **auto clipboard integration**.

---

## Features

1. **Clipboard Access** – `setclipboard`  
---
2. **Base64 Encode & Decode**  
---
3. **Byte Encode & Decode** (obfuscation)  
---
4. **Reverse & De-Reverse Text**  
---
5. **HWID Retrieval**  
---
6. **Executor Detection**  
---
7. **Custom Asset Loading**  
---
8. **Queue on Teleport (QOT)**  
---
9. **Clone References** (`cloneref`)
---
10. **Save Game Instance**  
---
11. **Message Box Notifications**  
---
12. **File & Folder Utilities** (read/write/create)

---

## 💡 Notes

- **Works in Lua/Luau executor environments**  
- **Clipboard integration included for all output functions**  
- **Some functions require executor support (e.g., `gethwid`, `identifyexecutor`)**
